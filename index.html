About me

I am an engineering student in Computer Science at ENSEIRB-MATMECA, one of the 8 graduate schools of Bordeaux Institute of Technology. I got into ENSEIRB after 2 years of studies in another engineering school : INSA Toulouse.

What I love most about computer science is that, on top of being interested per se, it is the most multipurpose and powerful tool I can think of. It is my intellectual curiosity, and my will to keep my interests as diversified as possible that pushed me into it.

Aerospace, biology, medicine, robotics, artifical intelligence are so many fascinating application fields of computer science that I am sure I would enjoy working in.

If you want to learn more about my profile, feel free to read my CV (available in english, and in french).
